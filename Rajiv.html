Rajiv Pathnaik

President, Howrah Christian School

Rajiv Pathnaik stands as a beacon of hope and inspiration, carrying forward the noble mission left behind by his father. A visionary leader, he has dedicated his life to nurturing young minds and shaping the future of underprivileged children.

With unwavering determination and boundless compassion, he ensures that every child receives the highest quality education, instilling in them the values of integrity, perseverance, and excellence.

Under his transformative leadership, Howrah Christian School has flourished, growing into a sanctuary of learning where dreams take flight. He is not just an administrator but a mentor, guide, and a true champion of education.
