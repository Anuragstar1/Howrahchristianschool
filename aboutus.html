About Us

Our Vision

Our aim at Howrah Christian School is to help each child realize their unique potential by offering an integral education. We provide the best CBSE-affiliated education, fostering a nurturing environment for the holistic development of every student.

Our Mission

Our educational approach focuses on nurturing not just academic growth, but also the physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual development of the child. Through progressive education, we guide students to manifest their full potential, resulting in success and satisfaction in both individual and collective living.

Core Values

We are committed to providing a well-rounded education that goes beyond academic success. At Howrah Christian School, we emphasize discipline, respect, creativity, and community engagement, equipping students to thrive in a global society and become lifelong learners.

Inclusive Learning

We believe that every student is unique, and our curriculum is designed to cater to individual needs and abilities. Our approach fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, preparing students for future challenges in an ever-evolving world.
