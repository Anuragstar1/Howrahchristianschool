Suparna Banerjee - Principal, Howrah Christian School

Meet Our Principal - Suparna Banerjee

Suparna Banerjee is an inspiring leader and the heart of Howrah Christian School. With years of experience in education, she has transformed the school into a nurturing environment where both students and teachers thrive. Known for her guidance, leadership, and vision, she has made a lasting impact on the entire school community.

As Principal, Suparna Banerjee is a compassionate counselor and a dedicated mentor. She believes in guiding her teachers and students to reach their fullest potential. Her deep commitment to personal development and academic excellence makes her a role model for everyone she works with.

Besides being an outstanding educator, Suparna Banerjee is a prolific author who has penned several books on education, leadership, and personal growth. Her written works provide valuable insights and inspire both educators and students alike.

Her Latest Book: "The Mixed Bag"

"The Mixed Bag" by Suparna Banerjee is an incredible collection of short stories and poems that captivates the hearts of its readers. It beautifully intertwines human emotions, life lessons, and creative expression. The book explores the intricacies of everyday life, with each story and poem offering something relatable and insightful.

Readers from all walks of life will find the book easy to connect with, and its diverse themes make it an enjoyable read for both young and old. Whether you're a fan of storytelling or poetry, "The Mixed Bag" offers an enriching experience that speaks to the soul.
